How to use DrawBoard

The goal of DrawBoard is create a parallelogram with an inner circle.
Follow the steps below to get to known the tool:

  1. You are going to click in 3 different points inside the DrawBoard, each click will generate a red dot and a number;
  2. As soon as you finish the third click, an paralelogram will be shown and the inner circle will be created;
  3. You can check all information about what you drew on the left side, next to the board;
  4. You can move the dots freely, in order to see the infotmation being updated;
  5. You can reset the board any time, just click on the reset button positioned on the right bottom of the board.
  6. Have Fun!
